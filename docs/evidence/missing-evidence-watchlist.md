# Missing-evidence watchlist

This investigation reached a publishable interim conclusion, but it does not claim to close the full economic truth.

The missing-evidence watchlist shows what data could change, strengthen, or weaken the conclusion.

## Priority fronts

1. Current 2026 income/remuneration table by formality or household distribution.
2. Current retail scanner or supermarket basket data.
3. Current household expenditure/volume survey.
4. Current survey with explicit substitution questions.

## Rule

Future evidence can upgrade a claim only if it passes period, unit, coverage, and method requirements.
