# Not Collapse, Not Comfort

## Evidence for a selective household squeeze in the Dominican Republic

**Core thesis:** Not collapse. Not comfort. A selective squeeze.

[Versión en español](../)

This project investigates whether the Dominican economy is better described as collapse, comfort, or selective squeeze. The interim conclusion, within the limits of the evidence, is that the collapse narrative is not supported, but the comfort narrative is not supported either.

The strongest reading is a selective squeeze: macro resilience and formal-channel cushioning can coexist with food pressure, small-business stress, unresolved informal-worker exposure, and missing direct current evidence on household trade-down.
