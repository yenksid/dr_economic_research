# Methodology and limits

This project separates evidence, context, inference, and boundaries.

## Rules

1. Do not convert pressure into behavior.
2. Do not generalize formal data to all households.
3. Do not convert labor context into income proof.
4. Do not use 2025 context as 2026 proof.
5. Do not use price-only evidence as trade-down proof.
6. Do not convert absence in tested sources into global absence.

## Result

The thesis is not a partisan claim or a closed prediction. It is an interim conclusion within explicit limits:

**Not collapse. Not comfort. A selective squeeze.**
